Waves · Superposition

Wave Superposition

Two travelling waves and their sum. Match the frequencies to see beats vanish; set them one apart and watch the envelope breathe. Hover to read all three displacements.

Principle of superposition: y(x,t) = y₁(x,t) + y₂(x,t)
