About

A Physics and Mathematics educator — an engineer by training who builds the software he teaches with.

I teach Physics and Mathematics online to IB, GCSE, and K–12 students across India, the USA, and Canada, and I run NJ’s Learning Hub in Ahmedabad for JEE, NEET, and GUJCET preparation. My teaching leans heavily on interactive tools — GeoGebra, Desmos, and simulations — and on Generative AI for content and analytics.

I hold an M.E. in Machine Design and a B.Ed, and years of resolving competitive-exam doubts have shaped how I break down hard concepts into clear, student-friendly steps.

Alongside teaching, I design and build education software: interactive simulations, career-guidance tools, and exam-preparation systems grounded in real Gujarat curriculum and admissions data.
